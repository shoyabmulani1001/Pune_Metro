About Pune Metro

Pune Metro is a rapid transit system serving the city of Pune, Maharashtra. It aims to reduce traffic congestion and provide a fast, eco-friendly, and affordable mode of transport. The metro has multiple lines, including the Purple Line and Aqua Line, connecting important parts of the city.

Developed by Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited (MAHA-METRO), it plays a vital role in enhancing urban mobility and reducing travel time.
